What Is Loyverse Shopify Integration?

Loyverse Shopify Integration is a process that connects your Loyverse POS system with your Shopify store, allowing data to sync automatically between the two platforms. Instead of managing inventory, sales, and customer information manually in each system, integration ensures everything stays updated in real time or near real time.

Using SKUPlugs Loyverse Shopify integration, actions performed in one system—such as creating a sale in Loyverse or receiving an order in Shopify—are instantly reflected in the other.
